Coffee creamers

People enjoy their coffee differently. Some like it black whereas some like it with cream keeping thier health in mind as creamers contain a lot of sugar. Adding creamers to your coffee certainly tastes good but at your health and calorie cost. So, its better to try the alternatives. If you like your coffee black but with flavor, you can add sugar, a flavoring like cinnamon. If avoiding the creamers completely is your idea then, you can add almond or skim milk to your coffee, or add straight cream, half-and-half or whole milk.
